About Us


Located in the heart of Bali’s serene landscapes, Sanctuary Bali is a beacon for those seeking a transformative yoga experience in one of the world’s most enchanting environments. Our studios and retreat center are dedicated to the ancient disciplines of Hatha Yoga and Tantric philosophy, designed to harmonize body, mind, and spirit. At Sanctuary Bali, the lush surroundings are not just a backdrop but an integral part of your yoga journey. Our retreats offer immersive workshops that connect you deeply with the elemental powers of this tropical paradise. Each session is crafted to bring out the profound wisdom of traditional practices, facilitated in spaces that blend open-air tranquility with authentic Balinese aesthetics. Join us to explore spiritual practices in an idyllic setting, where every breath and movement brings you closer to inner peace and personal transformation. Discover Sanctuary Bali, where each visit enriches your life’s journey.
